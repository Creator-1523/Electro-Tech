
# Solar Tracker 

The solar tracker can be controlled automatically with the help of LightDependent Resistor (LDR) sensors.

## Components We used:

1. Ardunio UNO
2. Servo Motor
3. LDR 
4. Jumper Wires
5. 9V Battery
